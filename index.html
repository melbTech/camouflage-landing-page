Hikes

Yosemite Valley is a glacial valley in California's Sierra Nevada mountains. It is known for its granite cliffs, waterfalls, and diverse wildlife.

Zion National Park is located in southern Utah and is known for its stunning sandstone cliffs, canyons, and scenic hikes.

The Wave features a series of sandstone formations that have been sculpted over time by wind and water, creating a mesmerizing pattern of undulating waves.
